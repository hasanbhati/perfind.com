Who are we?

At PerFind, we are dedicated to helping skilled workers find the right job opportunities in Europe. Whether you're an experienced professional or looking to expand your career, we're here to guide you every step of the way.

Job Placement

We connect you with reputable employers in Europe, offering jobs that match your skills and experience in industries such as construction, manufacturing, and more.

Visa and Work Permit Assistance

Navigating international job requirements can be challenging. Our team helps you with the paperwork, including visa applications and work permits, making the process as smooth as possible.

Career Counselling

Professional guidance to help you choose the right career path and understand European work culture.

Pre-Departure Support

Before you begin your journey, we provide support with:

  • Travel arrangements
  • Accommodation assistance
  • Cultural orientation to help you adapt to life and work in Europe

Transparent Process

We believe in complete transparency. From job descriptions to salaries, work conditions, and employer details, we ensure you have all the information you need before making any decisions.

Dedicated Support Team

Our team is always available to assist you at every stage—whether it's preparing for interviews, understanding your contract, or addressing any concerns during your employment.
